Let's Be Honest

In the world of fashion, it's time for change. The fashion industry has long neglected its responsibility to people and the planet. The term "sustainability" has lost its meaning through overuse, and the greenest choice may be not to buy new clothes at all.

However, as creators of clothing, we're on a mission to ensure that our creations have a purpose beyond your wardrobe. We pledge that our garments won't end up in landfills. Instead, we encourage you to mend them, share them, and pass them on. Our pieces are made to withstand the test of time and can bring joy to not just one, but many.

Create memories, make informed choices, and embrace a life that's conscious and vibrant.

OUR MISSION

At Southville Sporting Club, we inspire a love for vintage athletics, nature, and Bristol's vibrant spirit through our sustainable clothing. We craft high-quality, eco-friendly garments designed to last. We measure value by both style and environmental impact, ensuring our customers get outstanding value.

At Southville Sporting Club, we take pride in looking after our team members within the company and across our supply chains worldwide. Our goal is to ensure that our clothing is worn for a long time and doesn't end up in landfills.

We prioritize fair working conditions and waste reduction throughout our supply chain, fostering respect and longevity. For us, a sale is complete when our products are worn out and recycled, reflecting our commitment to sustainability.

Thank you for joining us on this exciting journey. Explore our collection, discover the beauty of Bristol, and experience the authenticity of Southville Sporting Club.
